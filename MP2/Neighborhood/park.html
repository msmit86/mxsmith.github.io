Park


You're standing in a park. There are children running around on a distant hilly patch of grass. Some parents are relaxing on the benches several feet beside you. Familiar sounds of laughter, dogs barking, and low chatter fill the air.
What isn't familiar is the park itself. Or any of the shops across the street. Or how you even arrived at this location.

You feel unsettled. But at least this seems to be a safe area.
